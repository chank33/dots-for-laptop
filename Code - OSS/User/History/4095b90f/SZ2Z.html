Low kcal, high protein, maxiumum taste Beef Ragu

Description

The recipe is a blend of minimal spices with maximum effect.

BROAT Ragu ingredients

You will need:

  • Ground beef. 500g
  • Chopped/Tinned Tomatos. 1 Can / 240g
  • Brown or Red Onion. 300g 1 or Large
  • MSG. 5-10g
  • Salt. To taste
  • Pepper. To taste
Optional, depending on the mood
  • Cumin
